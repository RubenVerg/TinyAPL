---
glyph: '~'
pattern: 'r←x~y'
name: Difference
---

`r` is the difference of the major cells of `x` and `y`, i.e. the cells of `x` that don't appear in `y`: `r←x⌿⍨~x∊y`. The difference of two dictionaries is the dictionary with keys that appear in `x` but not in `y`. Supports the `tolerance` extra argument for the comparison.
