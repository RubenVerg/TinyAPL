---
glyph: '∩'
pattern: 'r←x∩y'
name: Intersection
---

`r` is the intersection of the major cells of `x` and `y`, i.e. the cells of `x` that appear in `y`: `r←x⌿⍨x∊y`. The intersection of two dictionaries is the left-biased intersection of the entries. Supports the `tolerance` extra argument for the comparison.
